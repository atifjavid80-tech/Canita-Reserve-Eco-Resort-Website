Regenerative by design — SunDrop Canita merges luxury eco-tourism with active rainforest restoration. Located near Lake Bayano in the Chepo district, our project offers middle-income expats and eco-conscious travelers a rare synergy of high-end infrastructure, organic farm-to-table living, and direct environmental impact.

🌱 Our Rainforest, Your Paradise: 55 EcoHomes, 25 indigenous-inspired glamping sites, community gardens, natural spring lakes, holistic spa, and cooperative livestock production. Every stay or home ownership directly funds our 15-year reforestation program.

The Land

Easily accessible from the Pan-American Highway via Chepo, the 20-acre residential property offers stunning views of the surrounding rainforest and Lake Bayano.

  • 20 acres master-planned EcoVillage
  • 17 hectares active reforestation
  • 3 natural spring-fed lakes
  • 100% off-grid solar + micro-hydro

The EcoVillage & Resort

The BLOC campus includes senior care facility, language-immersive preschool, business center, social club, theater, restaurant, coffee shop, and market.

  • 55 EcoHomes + 60 rental apartments
  • 25 luxury glamping sites on Airbnb
  • Agroforestry: cocoa, avocado, mango, hardwoods
  • Co-op livestock & trout farming

Investment Highlights

Projected revenue from $3.86M (Year 1) to $16.2M (Year 5). EcoHome starting at $160,000 with deposits from $35,000.

Pathway to Panama Citizenship: Ownership includes legal services and residency-to-citizenship track within 5 years.

Off-Grid Resilience

Solar array + micro-hydro + battery storage · Greywater recycling · Electric ATVs & community SUVs · Starlink internet.
