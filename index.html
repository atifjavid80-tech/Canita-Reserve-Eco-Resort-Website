Regenerative by design — Sundrop Canita merges luxury eco-tourism with active rainforest restoration. Located near Lake Bayano in the Chepo district, our project offers middle-income expats and eco-conscious travelers a rare synergy of high-end infrastructure, organic farm-to-table living, and direct environmental impact.

🌱 Our Rainforest, Your Paradise: 55 EcoHomes, 25 indigenous-inspired glamping sites, community gardens, natural spring lakes, holistic spa, and cooperative livestock production. Every stay or home ownership directly funds our 15-year reforestation program.

AgroForestry & Restoration

Over 10,000 native trees + cocoa, avocado, mango, and exotic hardwoods planted on previously deforested land. Our integrated model produces timber, organic crops, and carbon credits while reviving biodiversity.

  • 2,000+ trees & crops
  • Carbon sequestration tracking
  • Co-op farming & trout production

EcoVillage Community

The BLOC campus includes senior care, language-immersive preschool, business center, social club, cinema, and commercial kitchen. Residents enjoy 21 farm-to-table meals/week, personalized assistance, and electric mobility.

  • 3 natural spring lakes
  • Fitness & yoga center
  • 24/7 private security

Investment Highlights

Projected revenue from $3.86M (Year 1) to $16.2M (Year 5). EcoHome deposits from $35,000 (10-20% of purchase price). Diversified income: home sales, glamping (ADR $140), agroforestry, and carbon credits.

Off-Grid Resilience

Solar array + micro-hydro + battery storage · Greywater recycling · Black water composting · Electric ATVs & community SUVs · Starlink high-speed internet · Helicopter taxi pad from Panama City.

Gateway to Eastern Panama

Lake Bayano & Caves

Boat trips, kayaking through canyons, limestone caves, and tarpon fishing. Only 20 minutes from Canita.

Mamoni River

Class III-IV whitewater rafting, year-round, 30 minutes away, through the Mamoni Preserve.

Cocobolo Nature Reserve

Over 300 bird species, primary forest hiking, and stargazing due to low light pollution.

Panama City & Casco Antiguo

UNESCO World Heritage sites, modern amenities, and Tocumen International Airport just 1.5 hours away.
