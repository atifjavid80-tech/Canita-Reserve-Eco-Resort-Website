Regenerative by design — Sundrop Resort at Canita Reserve merges agroforestry, luxury hospitality and active forest restoration. Located in the Chepo district near Lake Bayano, our project offers a unique value: middle-income expats and eco-conscious travelers can own a piece of the rainforest while directly contributing to carbon capture and biodiversity revival. Unlike rustic off-grid communities or overpriced private islands, we provide high-quality infrastructure, organic farm-to-table living, and a clear environmental impact.

AgroForestry & Restoration

Over 10,000 native trees and commercial crops (cacao, lime, avocado, tropical hardwoods) planted on 17 hectares of previously deforested land. Our 15-year reforestation program guarantees real impact — guests and residents participate in the revival of the Panamanian jungle.

  • 2,000+ trees & plants integrated
  • Carbon sequestration & biodiversity monitoring
  • Cooperative livestock & farm-to-fork

EcoHomes & Glamping Experience

55 EcoHomes spread across 20 acres (1/3 acre per dwelling) + 25 indigenous-inspired glamping sites. Enjoy private terraces, smart solar systems, and community amenities including natural spring lakes, holistic spa, fitness center, and farm cooperative. Each home includes 15 hours/week personalized assistance (childcare, executive assistant, forest guiding).

Lake Bayano & limestone caves

Explore beyond the resort

Lake Bayano: boat trips, kayaking through canyons, and extensive cave systems. Mamoni River: class III-IV whitewater rafting just 30 minutes away. Cocobolo Nature Reserve: birdwatching with over 300 species. The historic Casco Antiguo and Panama City are within easy reach — yet you return to the serenity of the rainforest.

Chepo & Canita serve as gateway to wilder Panama: mountain biking, indigenous culture, and world-class tarpon fishing at Coquira marina. Whether you’re a digital nomad, retiree, or nature enthusiast, Sundrop Resort provides the perfect base for authentic adventures.

Wellness & longevity: On-site organic produce, agroforestry workshops, spa, and spring-fed lakes for swimming and fishing.

The EcoVillage: holistic living, redefined

The EcoVillage Project at Canita Reserve features a complete community ecosystem: The BLOC campus building with senior care, language-immersive child care, business center, social club, and commercial kitchen. Residents enjoy family-style meals (21 meals/week delivered), community gardens, sports courts, and cooperative livestock production.

Fractional ownership — smart entry

Own a deeded share of an EcoHome with a lower capital commitment. Inspired by Pacaso & luxury residence clubs, our tech-enabled platform makes co-ownership seamless. Includes property management, rental income potential, and access to resort amenities. Starting deposits from 10-20% of home price.
